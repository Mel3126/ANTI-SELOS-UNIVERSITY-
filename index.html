University Map Direction :

Anti Selos University - Manila Campus 123 Beauty Boulevard Makati, Manila, Philippines 12345

By Car: From Ninoy Aquino International Airport: Exit the airport and follow the signs to the Skyway. Get on Skyway and take the Makati City exit. Drive along EDSA and turn right onto Ayala Avenue. Continue on Ayala Avenue, then turn left onto Beauty Boulevard. You'll find the university on your right. From Downtown Manila: Head south on EDSA and take the Ayala Avenue exit. Turn right onto Ayala Avenue, then left onto Beauty Boulevard. The campus will be on your right.

By Public Transit: MRT (Metro Rail Transit): Take the MRT Line 3 to Ayala Station. Exit the station and head east on Ayala Avenue. Turn left onto Beauty Boulevard, and you'll see the campus on your right. Jeepney: If you're taking a jeepney, look for one heading to Ayala or Makati Avenue. Ask the driver to drop you off near Beauty Boulevard. From there, it's a short walk to the university.

By Foot or Bike: If you're in the Makati area, you can easily walk or bike to Anti Selos University. Head to Ayala Avenue and then turn onto Beauty Boulevard.
